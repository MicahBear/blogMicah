Starting Again

January 29th, 2022

So.... I probably like many have had the bug(haha) for developing for some time now. I had the opportunity to learn from a friend who is a great Software Engineer. Learning from him for the few months we had was great but of course his work took off and rightfully so. I felt behind, not capable, ... blah blah. The usual self-doubt and thus reasoning with yourself that you should probably do something else with your time, give up, or move on.

I went back to farming, quick info: I have been a farmer for about 5+ years and now I just farm on my own 1/4 with my partner, that quickly didn't work out as I was looking for something else and maybe it was the cumulation of working with people I had worked with already and that I honestly hadn't given my full commitment to this process and realizing it is about growth and one step at a time.
